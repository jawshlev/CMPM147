Technical

Relation to inspring image:

  • Color: The sky, grass, tree, and stone colors were sampled from the inspiring image using the Photoshop eyedropper tool.
  • Primitives: The trees are rendered with ellipse primitives, and the grass is rendered with one large green rectangle.
  • Polygons:The waves are rendered via vertex calls, and animated by taking into account MouseX causing wave like changes in the sine graph. The terrace itself is made with vertex calls and is placed within a for loop that creates 10 seperate terraces out of vertexes.
  • Composition: Different random seed values lead to differently shaped terraces, and tree placement.
  • Life: The waves move based on the position of mouseX

index.html code borrowed from Professor Wes, ChatGPT helped in the creation of the base code, and the mouseX implementation
